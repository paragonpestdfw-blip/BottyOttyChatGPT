Multi-step tasks allow a single task to be worked on by multiple people in sequence, with each person adding their part before passing it along.

Example Scenario:

  1. Matt Kiger gets customer question
  2. Assigns to Presley for initial research
  3. Presley adds notes: "Customer wants commercial pricing"
  4. Presley passes to Sales team
  5. Sales provides quote and completes task

ğŸš€ How to Use Multi-Step

1 Work on your part of the task

2 Click ğŸ”— Pass to Next Person

3 Select who should receive it

4 Add handoff notes explaining what you did

6 Next person receives with full history

  4. Try manual refresh (click refresh button)

  6. Verify network connection
